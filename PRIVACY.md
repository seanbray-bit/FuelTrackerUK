# Privacy Policy - Fuel Tracker UK

**Last updated: 6 April 2026**

## Overview
Fuel Tracker UK ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Location Data
- We use your device's GPS location to find fuel stations near you.
- Location data is processed on your device and is not stored on our servers.
- You can disable location access at any time in your device Settings.

### Purchase Data
- If you subscribe, Apple handles all payment processing.
- We do not have access to your payment details.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect your name, email, or phone number.
- We do not track your browsing activity.
- We do not sell or share any personal data with third parties.
- We do not use advertising trackers or analytics SDKs.

## Third-Party Services
- **Apple StoreKit**: Used for subscription management. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
- **Fuel price data providers**: We fetch fuel price data from public/third-party sources. No personal data is sent to these services.

## Data Retention
We do not store any personal data. Location data is used in real-time only and is not retained.

## Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
